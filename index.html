Things To Do

Experiences & Activities

Immerse yourself in the farm lifestyle with hands-on experiences and outdoor adventures.

🔥

Campfire Nights

Gather around the fire under a star-filled sky. Share stories, roast snacks, and unwind in the warm glow of an open campfire.

🐇

Farm Animal Interaction

Get up close with our friendly rabbits, chicks, turkeys, and farm dogs. A delightful experience for kids and adults alike.

🌾

Rice Paddy Experience

Walk through lush rice paddies and learn about traditional farming. Reconnect with the roots of rural Tamil Nadu.

🥾

Nature Walks

Explore the 5-acre property on guided nature walks. Breathe in the fresh air and discover the farm's hidden green corners.

⛺

Camping

Set up a tent and spend a night under the open sky. Experience the magic of farm camping with all the essentials provided.

🍽️

Farm-to-Table Dining

Savour home-cooked meals prepared with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Authentic South Indian cuisine made with love.
